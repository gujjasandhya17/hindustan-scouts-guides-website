Organizational Structure

Executive Committee (65 Members)

The organization operates through a 65-member executive committee elected for a triennial tenure (2023-2026). This diverse committee includes national leadership, state representatives, commissioners, and co-opted members who bring expertise from various fields including government, military, education, and community service.

National Level

National Chairman oversees the organization with support from Chief Commissioner, Secretary, Joint Secretary, and various commissioners managing different portfolios including operations, international relations, and programs.

State Level

State Secretaries and Representatives lead regional operations, ensuring HSGA's mission is implemented effectively across different states and territories, adapting to local contexts while maintaining national standards.

Governance

The organization follows democratic principles with regular elections every three years. All positions are elected by members, ensuring accountability, transparency, and inclusive decision-making processes.

Special Positions

Co-opted members bring specialized expertise in areas like government liaison (IAS officers), community development, and organizational strategy, strengthening the executive committee's capabilities.

Leadership Principles

Visionary Leadership

Our leaders are committed to advancing HSGA's mission of youth empowerment while adapting to contemporary challenges and opportunities in the 21st century.

Democratic Governance

Leadership positions are earned through democratic elections, ensuring that leaders have the mandate and support of the member base, promoting accountability and transparency.

Service Orientation

All leaders operate under the founding motto "Serve Man to Reach God," prioritizing the welfare and development of youth and communities they serve.

Collaborative Approach

Leadership works collaboratively across regions and departments, fostering unity, shared learning, and collective success in advancing HSGA's objectives.

Continuous Learning

Leaders are committed to ongoing development, staying updated with contemporary approaches in youth development, education, and organizational management.

Inclusive Representation

The leadership structure ensures representation across regions, genders, and expertise areas, reflecting India's diversity and strengthening organizational reach and impact.
