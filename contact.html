Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join HSGA?

To join HSGA, contact our office during business hours or fill out the contact form. We will guide you through the registration process and enrollment requirements based on your age and interests.

What are the membership fees?

Membership fees vary based on age group and programs. Contact our office for detailed information about membership packages and payment options.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes, HSGA offers scholarships and support for deserving candidates. Contact our office for eligibility criteria and application procedures.

When are training programs conducted?

Training programs are conducted throughout the year during weekends and vacations. Annual camps are typically held during summer and winter breaks. Check our activities page for current schedule.

Can girls participate?

Yes, HSGA welcomes both boys and girls. We have equal opportunities for all genders in training programs and leadership positions.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers to support our activities and community programs. Contact our office to discuss volunteering opportunities that match your skills.

Do you offer certifications?

Yes, we offer recognized certifications in various skills including first aid, outdoor skills, and leadership training. Certificates are awarded upon successful completion of training.

How do we request your organization for speaking?

Fill out the contact form or call our office to discuss speaking engagements and outreach programs. Our representatives are available for schools and community events.
