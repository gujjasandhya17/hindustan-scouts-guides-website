Activities & Programs

Training Programs

🎯 Basic Training

Fundamental training modules covering basic scouting skills, first aid, rope techniques, and outdoor survival fundamentals for beginners.

⛺ Advanced Outdoor Skills

Comprehensive training in camping, trekking, water sports, rock climbing, wilderness survival, and specialized outdoor techniques.

👥 Leadership Development

Programs designed to develop leadership skills, team management, decision-making abilities, and motivational techniques for youth leaders.

🏥 First Aid & Medical Training

Certified first aid courses covering emergency response, basic medical knowledge, CPR, wound care, and health awareness programs.

💻 Digital Literacy & AI

Modern training in digital skills, cybersecurity awareness, responsible internet use, and introduction to artificial intelligence and emerging technologies.

🌟 Character Development

Programs focusing on value building, ethical education, discipline, patriotism, and personal development for holistic youth growth.

Community Service Activities

🌱 Environmental Conservation

Active participation in tree planting, waste management, water conservation, pollution control, and sustainable development initiatives.

🤝 Social Welfare Programs

Community outreach including education support for underprivileged children, health camps, senior citizen care, and disaster relief programs.

🛠️ Vocational Skills Training

Teaching practical vocational skills to youth, promoting self-employment, entrepreneurship, and economic self-reliance in communities.

📚 Literacy & Education

Facilitating adult literacy programs, scholarship support, educational mentoring, and learning opportunities for underprivileged sections.

⚕️ Health & Wellness Programs

Health awareness campaigns, nutrition education, hygiene promotion, disease prevention, and mental health wellness initiatives.

🆘 Disaster Management

Rapid response teams trained to assist during natural disasters, emergency evacuation, rehabilitation support, and community recovery.

National Events & Competitions

🏕️ National Annual Camp

Grand annual gathering bringing together scouts and guides from across India for 7-10 day outdoor camps with training, competitions, and cultural programs.

🏆 Sports & Athletic Competitions

National-level competitions in athletics, team sports, adventure challenges, and fitness events promoting healthy lifestyle and competitive spirit.

🎖️ Skill Testing & Badge Awards

Regular skill assessment programs and badge awarding ceremonies recognizing achievements in various outdoor, technical, and leadership competencies.

🎭 Cultural Programs

Celebration of Indian heritage through cultural performances, traditional music, dance, literature, and artistic expressions at national events.

🧠 Knowledge & Quiz Competitions

National level quiz competitions testing knowledge in history, geography, science, environmental awareness, and contemporary affairs.

🗳️ Triennial Elections & Conventions

Democratic elections every three years held at national convention, ensuring organizational governance and leader selection by member participation.

Youth Development Programs

🎓 Educational Support

Scholarship programs, exam preparation coaching, career guidance, mentoring by professionals, and educational counseling for youth from underprivileged backgrounds.

💡 Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Fostering entrepreneurial thinking, startup incubation support, innovation workshops, and business development training for young entrepreneurs.

🌍 International Exchange

International exposure programs, cultural exchange with scouts from other countries, and global networking opportunities for young leaders.

🎨 Arts & Creativity

Programs promoting creative arts, music, dance, visual arts, drama, and literary pursuits as avenues for expression and talent development.

🏃‍♂️ Health & Fitness

Comprehensive fitness programs, sports training, yoga and wellness practices, nutrition education, and mental health awareness initiatives.

🔬 STEM Education

Science, technology, engineering, and mathematics workshops, coding training, robotics clubs, and hands-on STEM learning experiences.
